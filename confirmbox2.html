HCL Digital Concerts – Unearthing India's Hidden Gems

Driven by the vision of identifying, nurturing and promoting the next generation maestros of Indian classical and fusion music, HCL Digital Concerts is a unique platform which will identify talented and aspiring musicians and provide them an opportunity to showcase their talent on digital as well as physical music concerts

How it works?

  1. Interested musicians/dancers can apply on this portal by submitting all required information and their music/dance performances.
  2. Once the portal is closed, all entries will be evaluated by our official talent screening partners Perfect Octave.
  3. 36 selected finalists will be announced on HCL Concerts social media channels.
  4. All selected finalists will be awarded the opportunity to perform at a digital concert presented by HCL Concerts.
  5. These performances will be evaluated by a panel of experts including accomplished Indian classical artists.
  6. Basis this evaluation, 8 winners will be announced on HCL Concerts social media channels over 12 months.
  7. These winners will get an opportunity to be mentored by the leading artists of today and perform in front of a live audience, along with veteran musicians/performers.

    8. ** The decisions of the talent screening partners and the panel of experts will be final and binding.

Rules for sharing music

  1. Each performance must not be greater than 15 minutes in duration.
  2. 1-2 songs must be studio recordings or live music. However, basic audio quality must be ensured.
  3. The songs can have a bit rate of 128 kbps, 160 kbps or 320 kbps and must not be more than 500 MB in total size.
  4. Do note that the receipt of any material by HCL doesn't guarantee a slot in the digital concerts or that it will be uploaded on the HCL Music app.
  5. The decision of the final performing artists will be at the sole discretion of the HCL, the official talent screening partners and the panel of experts.
